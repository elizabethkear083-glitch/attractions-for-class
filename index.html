Activities in the Perimiter

These destinations are within the perimiter of Atlanta, which is usually defined by being within the loop of highway 285. Most of these could be visited within a single day
  1. Georgia Aquarium
  2. Atlanta Botanical Gardens
  3. Center for Puppetry Arts
Many activities within the perimeter can be done with a City Pass, which is a one time bundle that has admission to most of the major activities within Georgia.

Activities outside the Perimeter

These are destinations outside the 285 loop that would be best done as a single day trip to each location on this list
  1. Helen, GA
  2. Stone Mountain, GA
  3. Ellijay Mushroom Farms

Seasonal Activities

In this list, there are some seasonal activities you can do across Georgia. These activities ususally happen once per year or best enjoyed during a specific season
  1. Georgia Renaissance Festival
